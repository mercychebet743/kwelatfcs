About Our Factory

Kwelat Coffee Factory is a private coffee processing factory established to deliver high-quality coffee to both local and international markets.

We work closely with farmers to ensure quality, sustainability, and consistency in every batch of coffee we produce.

Our Mission

To process and deliver premium coffee while supporting farmers and promoting sustainable coffee practices.

Our Vision

To be a leading coffee processing factory known for quality, integrity, and excellence.
